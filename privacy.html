Privacy & Security

Last updated: December 2025

Payment Link Verifier (PLV) runs entirely in your browser. When you check a link, the URL and any derived metadata remain in your device and are not sent to our servers by default.

What we do

PLV performs local, client-side heuristics to flag obvious indicators of phishing or scam links (suspicious keywords, non-HTTPS, known shorteners, unusual TLDs), and surface any detected Web3 addresses found in the URL.

Limitations

These checks are heuristic and not exhaustive. PLV does not connect to every external reputation service or guarantee safety. Use it as a quick triage tool, not a final arbiter.

Open Source & Data

The project's source code is available in the repository. You can inspect how checks are performed and run builds yourself. If you want to audit or contribute, please visit the GitHub repo.
